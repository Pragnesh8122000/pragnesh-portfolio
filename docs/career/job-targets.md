## 🔍 How to find MORE companies (the goldmine)

### LinkedIn search strings (use these)

1. **Open AI roles in India:**
   - `("AI Engineer" OR "GenAI Engineer" OR "LLM Engineer") AND (India OR Remote)`

2. **At specific tier:**
   - `"AI Engineer" "LangChain" "RAG"` (companies using your tools)
   - `"AI Engineer" "OpenAI" "Python"` (companies using OpenAI)

3. **Recruiter search:**
   - `("AI recruiter" OR "ML recruiter" OR "talent partner") AND "AI"`

### Job boards (in order of effectiveness)

1. **LinkedIn** — #1 for AI roles
2. **Indeed / Naukri** — for service cos
3. **Y Combinator's Work at a Startup** — startup roles, often remote
4. **Wellfound (AngelList)** — startup roles
5. **Himalayas** — remote jobs
6. **RemoteOK** — remote jobs
7. **AI-specific boards:**
   - AI/ML jobs on Hacker News "Who's Hiring" thread (monthly)
   - MLconf jobs board
   - Latent Space jobs board
   - AI-Jobs.net
8. **Company career pages directly** — often have unlisted roles
9. **Twitter/X job posts** — many AI jobs posted here first

## 🎯 The 4-step outreach to target companies

1. **Find the hiring manager** on LinkedIn: "[Company] Engineering Manager AI"
2. **Connect with a personalized note** (template in `personal-branding.md`)
3. **Apply on careers page** + DM: "I just applied for [role], would love to chat about how I could contribute"
4. **Engage with their content** for 2-3 weeks before applying (comment on posts, share their open source work)

**Conversion rate:** Cold apply = 1-3%. With warm outreach = 15-30%.

---

## ⚠️ Red flag companies to AVOID

- ❌ "Unlimited PTO" but no actual usage
- ❌ No clear AI/ML roadmap (you're joining to do AI)
- ❌ Founders who can't explain their AI differentiation
- ❌ Equity with 5+ year vest and no liquidity
- ❌ "We're like a family" (usually = overwork)
- ❌ Glassdoor reviews mentioning "used as ML labeling service"
- ❌ Pure consultancy ("we'll put you on any project")
