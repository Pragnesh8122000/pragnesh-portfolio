## 💡 Application best practices

1. **Always tailor your resume** — 5 mins per application = 50% higher response rate
2. **DM the hiring manager** — 24-48 hours after applying
3. **Send a thank-you note** within 24 hours of every interview
4. **Follow up once** if no response in 2 weeks (politely)
5. **Don't apply to the same role twice** — it shows desperation
6. **Apply within 48 hours of job posting** — early applicants win
7. **Apply on Tuesday-Thursday** — best response rates
8. **Use referrals when possible** — 4x higher response rate
